About Me

I am Sajjad Rahnama, Ph.D. Candidate at Department ofComputer Science of the University of California, Davis supervised by Prof. Mohammad Sadoghi. I’m also a member of Exploratory Systems Lab.My current research focuses on the geo-scale byzantine fault tolerant protocols, distributed systems, and their applications in blockchain and secure transaction processing. I am also system designer and main developer of ResilientDB. I got my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Amirkabir University of Technology, Tehran, Iran.
